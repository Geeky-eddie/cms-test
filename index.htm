Services we offer

At ENERGYSINTERIOR, we're here to make your home better. We offer quality products for security and style, and we can help with interior design to make your space just the way you like it. If you want something unique, we can create custom solutions for you. Our team of experts will handle installation and keep everything looking great. FAQ

1What types of tiles do you offer?

We offer a wide range of tiles, including ceramic, porcelain, glass, marble, granite, and more. You can explore our vast variety of products both in-store and online.

2Are your tiles suitable for outdoor use?

Some of our tiles are suitable for outdoor applications, while others are designed for indoor use. Please send us a message today for guidance based on your specific needs.

3Do you provide installation services?

Yes, we offer professional installation services for our products. Our experienced team will ensure that your tiles, wall panels, or doors are installed correctly.

4Do you offer discounts for bulk purchases?

Yes, we offer discounts for bulk purchases. Please contact our sales repersentative to discuss your specific requirements

5 What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, cash, and online transfers. You can choose the most convenient option for your purchase.

6 How can I place an order for online delivery?

You can easily place an order for online delivery through our website. Simply select the product you want, click PURCHASE and you will be directed to our sales representative, where you will provide your delivery details and we will take care of the rest.

7Where is your physical store located?

Our physical store is located at Plot 36, Rayino Plaza, after Panteker opposite Obumek Aluminum, Dei Dei int'l market, Abuja Nigeria. You can visit us during our operating hours to explore our products in person.
